Uhlelo Lwethu Lwemibala Lusebenza

Moenie baiza nie — inkulumo yase-township esho ukuthi ungehluleki, zima kuzwi lakho — iwumlando wezo ndawo ezifana naseEldorado Park, ezibekelwe isikhathi eside izibhamu negciwane lobugebengu. Umusi oshiyeka emuva kokudubulwa kwesibhamu waba yisimo: into ephilayo, yemvelo, eguqula ithuluzi lokulimaza ibe uphawu lwenqubekela phambili. Izithombe ezintathu ezingezansi zibonisa uhlelo lwemibala yamaHausa lusebenza kuleso simo — ngoba umbala awukaze unganaki. Uphatha ukukhumbula, ubunjalo, nencazelo, futhi ezandleni ezifanele ungaguqula okusindayo kube yinto ebamba abantu phezulu.
