Uhlelo Lwethu

Imibala Eyaqanjwa Kabusha

Umbala ngamunye waqanjwa kabusha ngokuhlanganisa amagama ezilimi ezimbili zaseAfrika, kuqagelwa incazelo ngaphezu kwembonakalo. Esikhundleni sokusebenzisa amagama emibala aseNtshonalanga, amagama asukelela ezinhlangeni zethu ezikhethiwe.

Tiziri-Duhu Omnyama

Incazelo Yegama

Tiziri (Amazigh: ubumnyama obuphethe ukukhanya)

Duhu (impande yesiSwahili: ubumnyama obuqinile)

Incazelo

Ubujule, imfihlakalo, ikhosmos, okhokho.

Fari-Timεt Omhlophe

Incazelo Yegama

Fari (Hausa: umhlophe/ubuhlanzekile)

Timεt (Amazigh: ubunjalo/ukuba)

Incazelo

Ukuhlanjululwa, ukucacisa ngokomoya, ukuba khona kokhokho. Umhlophe njengokulazi wena, hhayi ukungabikho.

Mogya-Ja Obomvu

Incazelo Yegama

Mogya (Akan: igazi)

Ja (Hausa: ubomvu/ukushisekela)

Incazelo

Igazi, amandla empilo, umnikelo, igunya. Umbala ohamba kuzo cishe zonke izindlela zemibala yaseAfrika njengendawo ebalulekile.

Sika-Warabe Omthubi

Incazelo Yegama

Sika (Akan: igolide/ingcebo)

Warabe (Oromo: ukukhanya/umgubho)

Incazelo

Igolide, isivuno, ilanga liphakeme, inala.

Kore-Uhlaza Oluhlaza

Incazelo Yegama

Kore (Hausa: uluhlaza/izitshalo)

Uhlaza (isiZulu/isiNdebele: uluhlaza/izinto eziphilayo)

Incazelo

Ukukhula, umhlaba, ukuphulukisa, konke okukhula emhlabathini.

Buluu-Amanzi Ubhuluu

Incazelo Yegama

Buluu (Hausa: ubhuluu, elaziwa futhi laziwe)

Amanzi (isiZulu/isiNdebele: amanzi)

Incazelo

Izulu, amanzi — ubhuluu noluhlaza njengensimu eyodwa ephilayo eqhubekayo.

Uhlelo Lwethu Lwesikhombisi

Uhlelo lwethu lwesikhombisi lusebenzisa incazelo yamasiko nenga-saikoloji ngokuqondile emibhalweni yemibala, lushintshe imodeli yeNtshonalanga ye-RGB ngezinhlaka ezinemizi ezinolwazi lweAfrika.

Isakhiwo — Inani Isiko Incazelo Umthombo

Inani

Inani likhombisa amandla embala. Omnyama, njengoumbala okhombisa amandla, uxutshaniswa nombala oyisisekelo wezinhlanga ngayinye ukhombisa ukuqina kwencazelo. Lokhu kudala uhla lwamatoni epaletini yezinhlanga ngayinye, kunikeza abayili ukusebenza ngobukhulu bamasiko ngaphandle kokwaphula uhlelo.

Isiko

Isizwe esisodwa esivela esifundeni ngasinye saseAfrika, sifinyezelwa ezinhlamvwini ezintathu.

Afrika Empumalanga ORM = Oromo
Afrika eNtshonalanga HSA = Hausa
Afrika Enyakatho AMZ = Amazigh
Afrika eNingizimu NDB = Ndebele
Afrika Emaphakathi BWA = Batwa

Incazelo

Izincazelo zahlelwa zaqanjwa izinombolo ngokulandela izikhombisi zezinombolo zaseAfrika.

Ukungqubuzana [2] Ukufutheka, Ingozi, Inhlanhla Embi, Ukweqa Umthetho
Imizwa [3] Ithemba, Ukuthula, Ukuzinza, Ukuvumelana, Inhlalakahle, Inala
Imvelo [4] Umhlaba, Imvelo, Isivuno, Ukukhanya Kwelanga, Ipharadisi, Izintaba, Ulwandle LweMediterranea
Ukuphila [5] Ukuphila, Amandla Empilo, Amandla, Ingcebo, Ukukhula, Ukuzala, Ukufudumala, Umgubho
Okomoya [7] Umphefumulo, Ubuhlanzekile, Imfihlakalo, Ukuba Nokomoya, Ukukhanya Kwaphezulu, Amandla Okomoya, Ukuhlanza Ngokomoya, Ukuba Khona Kokhokho, Ukuxhumana Ngokomoya
Amandla [8] Igazi, Igunya, Udumo, Umnikelo, Ukumelana Ngomlando
Ubukhosi [9] Ubunye, Ubunjalo, Isibindi, Ukusinda, Ubuqine, Ukuvikelwa, Ukubekezela, Ukwethembeka

Umthombo

Umbala ngamunye unemthombo wawo wenyama. Lemithombo yahlelwa ibe yiMakhe [M], Izitshalo [V], Insekto [I] neDaka [C].

M
Umakhe
Om-ochre, umusi, umhlabathi wotshe lwensimbi, amalahle, i-malachite
V
Izitshalo
I-indigofera, izikhumba ze-eniyana, amaqabunga omkhiwane, impande ye-madder, i-turmeric
I
Insekto
I-cochineal — isetshenziswa ngobomvu obuqinile kuzo zonke izindlela zaseAfrika Mpumalanga
C
Udaka
Udaka lwe-kaolin, itshaki — kusetshenziswa ngomhlophe, amakhrimu, ukupenda umzimba ngokomoya

Kungani Sabuya Sahlela Uhlelo Lwesikhombisi?

Njalo uma umyili esebenzisa umbala, basebenzisana nolwazi oluthile lwezinhlanga zaseAfrika, incazelo yengqondo neyokomoya, izindlela zomlando zokukhiqiza, nobujule bamatoni ngomongo wamasiko.

Zama!
